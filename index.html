Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Drop Pop ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address through the Game.

However, the Game may use third-party services that may automatically collect certain technical information, such as device information, operating system version, language settings, app usage data, crash data, advertising identifiers, or other diagnostic data.

2. Third-Party Services

Drop Pop may use third-party services such as Google Play Services, Unity services, analytics providers, and advertising providers. These services may collect limited technical data required for app functionality, performance monitoring, analytics, fraud prevention, and, if applicable, advertising.

These third-party services process data according to their own privacy policies.

3. How Information Is Used

Any data collected by third-party services may be used to:

  • provide and maintain app functionality;
  • improve game performance and stability;
  • analyze usage and fix technical issues;
  • prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity;
  • display ads, if advertising is enabled in the Game.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Limited technical data may be processed by third-party service providers that support app distribution, analytics, diagnostics, and advertising functionality.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not directly store personal user account data through the Game.

Any data processed by third-party providers is retained and deleted according to their own policies and legal obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

Drop Pop is a casual game intended for a general audience.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us and we will review the matter.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to use reliable platforms and service providers to help protect information processed in connection with the Game. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date at the top of this Privacy Policy will be revised accordingly.
